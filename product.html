The Oak Lounge is a single, continuous gesture — a looping bouclé form that cradles you from any angle, poised on a floating brushed-brass ring. Each chair is upholstered by hand in dense, temperature-friendly bouclé and swivels a full 360° on a concealed ball bearing. A quiet centrepiece that looks sculptural from every side of the room.
