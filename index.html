Care Guide

Built to last — if you look after it

Good furniture ages with you. A little care keeps it looking new for decades.

Wipe, don't soak

Dust with a dry or slightly damp soft cloth. Avoid standing water and harsh cleaners — they dull the finish over time.

Re-oil yearly

A yearly coat of hard-wax oil keeps the grain fed and the surface protected — the same finish we use in the workshop.

Keep it out of direct sun

Extended direct sunlight can unevenly fade oak — an occasional rotation keeps the colour consistent.
